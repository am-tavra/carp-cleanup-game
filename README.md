# 🐟 Carp Cleanup

**Save Our Waterways. Feed Our Pups.**

A strategy/management simulation game by [Archway Pet Food](https://archwaypetfood.com) that highlights the environmental impact of removing invasive Asian carp from American waterways — and turning them into hypoallergenic dog food and treats.

## Play

Deploy removal teams across 5 river zones, restore native ecosystems, and transform every catch into sustainable pet nutrition. Track both environmental recovery and pups fed healthier food across 12 rounds.

## Products Featured

- 🥣 Carp Kibble — Complete hypoallergenic dog food
- 🐟 Minced Carp Chips — High-protein single-ingredient chips
- 🍪 Carp Biscuits — Crunchy hypoallergenic treats
- 🦴 Rolled Carp Skins — Long-lasting natural chew
- 🫧 Air Bladder Chews — Novel protein, zero waste

## Deploy

This project is configured for one-click deployment on Vercel:

1. Push to GitHub
2. Import the repo in [vercel.com/new](https://vercel.com/new)
3. Vercel auto-detects Vite — just click Deploy
